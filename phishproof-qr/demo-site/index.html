What problem are we solving?

QR codes hide the destination until after a scan. That makes phishing attacks easy to disguise as menus, parking meters, tickets, and event links.

What makes this different?

Instead of immediately opening the QR destination, PhishProof QR explains why a link looks safe or suspicious and lets the user continue from the same screen.

How does scanning work?

The product is designed around iPhone usage first. In the browser demo, we simulate the scan flow and support camera-based photo capture so the experience feels closer to mobile Safari.

What is the hackathon hook?

It combines a strong cybersecurity story, a visual reveal, and a simple demo flow judges can understand in seconds.
