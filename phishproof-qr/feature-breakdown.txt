Feature Breakdown

Must-have MVP
1. Live iPhone camera-based QR scanning
2. Image upload QR scanning
3. QR payload decoding
4. QR payload classification
5. Redirect expansion
6. Domain risk analysis
7. Verdict screen
8. Risk explanation bullets
9. Manual approved action button

Supported approved actions
1. Open website
2. Copy text
3. Start phone call
4. Open email compose
5. Open SMS compose
6. Preview Wi-Fi details
7. Preview contact card

Auto-execute rules
1. Only for Safe verdicts
2. Only when enabled by the user
3. Only for approved action types
4. Disabled for risky or ambiguous payloads

Should-have after MVP
1. Scan history
2. Copy URL
3. Share result
4. Suspicious-domain education tips
5. Trusted allowlist
6. Secure in-app browser preview
7. Auto-open low-risk website actions

Nice-to-have later
1. Threat-intel backend
2. Family or enterprise mode
3. Browser extension companion
4. Apple Watch alert handoff
5. Branded QR verification system
6. Learned risk model trained on malicious and benign QR destinations
