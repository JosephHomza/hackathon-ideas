Hardware Ideas

1. PillPing
Concept
A simple smart pill reminder box with lights, buzzer alerts, and a web or phone dashboard.

Why judges may love it
Physical demo, clear usefulness, and healthcare angle.

Why it is easy enough
Basic sensors, timers, LEDs, and simple status tracking are very hackathon-friendly.

MVP
- reminder light or sound
- button to confirm medication taken
- basic dashboard
- daily log

Wow factor
A real object helps solve a real daily problem.

2. ClaimScan Station
Concept
A document scanning station for clinics or offices that captures paperwork and turns it into structured digital tasks.

Why judges may love it
Physical and software blend, practical workflow impact.

Why it is easy enough
Use a camera module or phone plus simple OCR pipeline and dashboard.

MVP
- scan paper document
- extract title and due date
- create digital task card
- show on dashboard

Wow factor
Paper chaos becomes organized instantly.

3. QuietLight
Concept
A desk light that changes color based on room noise and helps classrooms or shared workspaces stay calm.

Why judges may love it
Very visual, instant feedback, easy to understand.

Why it is easy enough
Microphone sensor, LEDs, and a simple threshold system.

MVP
- detect room volume
- color feedback
- simple calm score
- optional logging

Wow factor
The whole room can see and react to the system live.
