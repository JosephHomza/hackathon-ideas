Hello World Ideas

1. Denial Decoder
Concept
Upload a healthcare denial letter and get a simple summary, due date, and next-step checklist.

Why judges may love it
It solves a real problem but is still understandable and beginner-friendly.

Why it is easy enough
One upload flow, simple extraction, and a clean results screen.

MVP
- upload one PDF
- extract denial reason
- show due date
- generate next-step list

Wow factor
Messy paperwork becomes a clear action plan in seconds.

2. Resume Rewriter for First Jobs
Concept
A beginner-friendly app that turns a rough resume into cleaner bullet points for internships and entry-level jobs.

Why judges may love it
Relatable, useful, and easy to test live.

Why it is easy enough
Text input and text transformation is manageable in a hackathon.

MVP
- paste resume text
- choose target role
- get improved bullet points
- copy/export results

Wow factor
Before and after resume transformation is instant and obvious.

3. Study Sprint Buddy
Concept
A simple web app that creates a mini study plan from a topic and available time.

Why judges may love it
It is easy to understand and useful for students.

Why it is easy enough
It is mostly form input, planning logic, and polished presentation.

MVP
- input topic
- input time available
- generate study plan
- progress checklist

Wow factor
A vague goal becomes a clear plan in one click.
