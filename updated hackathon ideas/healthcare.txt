Healthcare Ideas

1. ClaimBridge
Concept
An AI assistant that turns denial letters and claim paperwork into a clear appeals workflow for healthcare billing teams.

Why judges may love it
Strong healthcare fit, real admin pain point, and measurable impact.

Why it is easy enough
The MVP can be document upload, extraction, checklist creation, and timeline display.

MVP
- upload denial letter
- extract payer and denial reason
- show missing requirements
- generate appeal checklist
- deadline tracker

Wow factor
Dense healthcare paperwork becomes a clear path to action.

2. VisitPrep
Concept
A patient-facing tool that helps people prepare for doctor visits by organizing symptoms, questions, medications, and history into a single summary.

Why judges may love it
Patient-centered, easy to demo, and broadly relatable.

Why it is easy enough
Mostly structured input plus smart summarization and export.

MVP
- symptom and question input
- medication list
- visit summary output
- printable doctor handoff sheet

Wow factor
A messy set of thoughts becomes a clean visit-ready brief.

3. MedTranslate
Concept
A simple app that rewrites medical instructions into clearer everyday language for patients.

Why judges may love it
Strong healthcare communication angle and obvious usefulness.

Why it is easy enough
Text simplification is a manageable scope for a hackathon.

MVP
- paste medical instructions
- rewrite in plain language
- show reading level
- translate to another language if time allows

Wow factor
Confusing medical language becomes understandable instantly.
